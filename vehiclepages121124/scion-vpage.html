Here are three Scion models we love with recommended maintenance:

Scion tC

Short for "touring coupe," the tC is Scion's compact coupe with an aggressively sporty design and a panoramic sunroof. Keep it in top condition with regular oil changes and filter checks.

Scion xB

For a more aggressive design profile, look to the xB. The XB is Scion's utility vehicle with a distinctive boxy design, roomy interior, and impressive interior and exterior upgrade options. Knock it out of the park by referring to your owner's model for the recommended maintenance schedule.

Scion iQ

One of Scion's most popular models, the iQ, is the smallest vehicle in the lineup. It offers comfortable seating (with a 3+1 seating configuration), impressive maneuverability for tight parking spots, and impressive fuel economy. Recommended maintenance for the iQ indicates regular oil changes, brake inspections, tire rotations, and fluid checks.
